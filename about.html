About CalcLabz

Free tools for content creators

CalcLabz is a free online calculator platform built for content creators. Our tools help YouTubers, bloggers, and social media creators estimate their earnings instantly.

We provide accurate estimates based on real RPM data across different niches and countries — so you can plan your content strategy better.

Our Mission

To help every creator — especially Indian creators — understand their earning potential and grow their channels with confidence.
