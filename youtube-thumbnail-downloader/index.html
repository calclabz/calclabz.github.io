What is a YouTube Thumbnail?

A YouTube thumbnail is the preview image displayed before a video starts playing. It is the first thing viewers see when browsing YouTube. A good YouTube thumbnail image directly affects how many people click on your video.

YouTube recommends thumbnails in 1280x720 pixels (HD) with a 16:9 aspect ratio. Using our free YouTube thumbnail grabber, you can download any public video's thumbnail in HD quality for free.
