YouTube RPM vs CPM — What's the Difference?

CPM (Cost Per Mille) is what advertisers pay YouTube per 1000 ad impressions. RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is what YOU actually earn per 1000 views after YouTube takes its 45% cut.

For Indian creators in 2026, average CPM ranges from ₹25 to ₹300, while RPM is typically ₹15 to ₹170 depending on niche.

YouTube Shorts vs Long Videos — Which Pays More?

Long-form videos pay significantly more than YouTube Shorts. In India, YouTube Shorts RPM is approximately ₹5-₹30 per 1000 views, while long videos earn ₹50-₹200. However, Shorts help grow your channel faster!

Tips to Increase YouTube Earnings in India

  • 🎯 Target international audience — US/UK viewers pay 5-10x more than Indian viewers
  • 💰 Choose high RPM niche — Finance and Technology pay highest in India
  • ⏱️ Make videos 8+ minutes — Unlocks mid-roll ads for more revenue
  • 📅 Upload more in Q4 — October-December has highest CPM rates (40-80% more!)
  • 🤝 Get brand sponsorships — Often pays 2-5x more than AdSense alone
